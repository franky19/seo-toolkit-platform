### Service Availability Policy for PlugFlow (SEO Toolkit Platform)

**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026

This Service Availability Policy outlines PlugFlow's commitment to the uptime and accessibility of our SEO Toolkit Platform ("Service"), operated as an independent online business by an individual in Indonesia. While we strive for maximum uptime, certain factors are beyond our control.

**1. Uptime Goal**

We aim to provide a high level of availability for our Service. Our goal is to achieve 99.5% uptime for the core analysis functionality of the SEO Toolkit Platform, measured monthly. This excludes scheduled maintenance and factors beyond our reasonable control.

**2. Scheduled Maintenance**

To ensure the optimal performance, security, and updates of our Service, we may perform scheduled maintenance. We will endeavor to:

*   Schedule maintenance during off-peak hours (typically weekends or late evenings, Western Indonesian Time).
*   Provide advance notice of scheduled maintenance (e.g., via email, in-app notification, or website announcement) whenever possible.
*   Minimize the duration of any service interruptions.

**3. Unscheduled Outages**

In the event of an unscheduled outage, we will:

*   Work diligently to identify the cause and restore Service functionality as quickly as possible.
*   Provide updates on the status of the outage if it is prolonged.

**4. Exclusions from Uptime Goal**

The following events are not included in our uptime calculation and are considered outside of our control:

*   **Force Majeure:** Events such as natural disasters, acts of war or terrorism, civil disturbances, government actions, or other events beyond our reasonable control.
*   **Third-Party Services:** Issues arising from third-party services (e.g., internet service providers, cloud hosting providers, external APIs, payment gateways) that are not directly managed by PlugFlow.
*   **User-Side Issues:** Problems originating from your internet connection, devices, software, or your actions.
*   **Malicious Attacks:** Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks or other malicious activities aimed at disrupting the Service.
*   **Beta/Experimental Features:** Uptime guarantees do not apply to features or services explicitly designated as beta, experimental, or free-tier.

**5. Remedies for Service Unavailability**

While we do not offer Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with specific compensation for downtime for all users, if a prolonged and unresolvable service unavailability occurs for paid subscribers that falls outside the exclusions, you may be eligible for an exception to our Refund Policy (as detailed in the Refund Policy). Such remedies are provided at our sole discretion.

**6. Monitoring and Reporting**

We utilize monitoring tools to track the performance and availability of our Service. We may provide status updates on our website or through communication channels if there are significant widespread issues.

**7. Changes to This Policy**

We reserve the right to modify this Service Availability Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. Your continued use of the Service after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
